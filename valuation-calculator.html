Understanding Valuation

How Business Valuation Works

SDE Multiple Method

The most common method for small businesses. Multiplies Seller's Discretionary Earnings by an industry-specific factor. SDE represents the true economic benefit to an owner-operator.

Revenue Multiple Method

Useful for high-growth businesses or those with inconsistent profitability. Based on a percentage of annual revenue, adjusted for industry and growth trajectory.

Key Value Drivers

Multiples are adjusted based on: revenue trends, owner dependency, customer concentration, market position, recurring revenue, and operational systems.
